What is m2w for?

The main idea of M2W is to help people to remember all movies
they ever heard, seen a trailer or were advised
by other people to definitely watch. If movie was good,
user can advise it to friends.

What can You do here?

1.Find movies to watch
2. Create Your own list to watch
3. Add friends to know their preferences
4. Advice film to friends

How it works?

1. Create an account
2. Find the film You want to see
3. Add it to Your wishlist
4. Mark it watched
5. Advice to Friends
